Retail

The Retail industry vertical domain describes the specific data requirements for the procurement, management, selling and delivery of goods and services to retail customers. It is divided in to sub-domains covering:

  • Selling - the transacting with retail customers for the sale of Products (goods and services)
  • LogicalSupplyChain - the planning and management of the flow of goods through the whole of the supply chain
  • PhysicalSupplyChain - the physical movement of goods through the whole of the supply chain
