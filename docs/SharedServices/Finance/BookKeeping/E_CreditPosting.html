CreditPosting extends LedgerEntry

The CreditPosting entity is a concrete sub class of the abstract Posting entity such that a JournalEntry (i.e. an Accounting Transaction) can be declared to have at least one Credit element in accordance with the requirements of double entry bookkeeping.
