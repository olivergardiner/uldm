ControlAccount extends Account
 implements Common::Base::ReferenceData::IControlledVocabulary

A control Account is a summary Account where the transaction detail is maintained in a separate Ledger and the Control Account is updated with the summary information on a periodic basis. While the Control Account will always balance from a double entry point of view, it may or may not be considered authoritative as the definitive view must derive from either the Control Account or the Subsidiary Ledger but not both.
