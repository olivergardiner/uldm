A Segment is a leaf node within a Segment Hierarchy and so its identity represents a value that can be used to classify an accounting transaction such that costs may be tracked within the accounting system. In many cases, the identity of the Segment may be overloaded as the identity of a corresponding Account but it is more accurate to separate their identities (noting that not all Segment Hierarchy nodes are Segments) and so each Segment references the identity of the Account that is used to accrue its costs.
